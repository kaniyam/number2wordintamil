Number2WordinTamil

Tool to Convert Numbers to their word equivalent in Tamil.

Description

There are several tools available online that will convert a number into its word equivalent in English but there is no such tool available for converting numbers to Tamil word equivalent. So, i decided to write such tool that will convert numbers to their word equivalent in Tamil.

It can convert upto 9999999999 i.e., 10 digits. The tool is available in two languages. One in English which will give outputs like “Nooru”, “Aayiram” for those don’t have Tamil Font support in their systems. The other in Tamil which will give outputs in Tamil itself for those who have Tamil Font Support. It also displays numeral equivalent in Tamil.
